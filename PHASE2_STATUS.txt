DAILY DISTRIBUTION FORMULA:
  Total Pool = 2,739.72 + (user_count / 1,000) × 27.4
  User Share = (user_daily_rp / total_network_rp) × Total Pool

EXAMPLE (3 users):
  Total RP: 13 (2 + 5 + 6)
  Total Pool: 2,739.80
  User A: (2/13) × 2,739.80 = 421.51 tokens
