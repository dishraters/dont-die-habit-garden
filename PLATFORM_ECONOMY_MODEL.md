## Token Demand From Developers

### Scenario: 100 Developers Launching Apps

**Year 1 Calculation:**

```
Developer token purchases (Year 1):

100 developers × 565K tokens (launch) = 56.5M tokens
100 developers × 110K tokens/month × 12 months = 132M tokens

TOTAL DEVELOPER DEMAND: 188.5M tokens in Year 1
```

**This is 47% of the 400M user reward tokens.**

**What this means:**
- 400M tokens issued to users
- 188.5M tokens purchased by developers (from secondary market or directly)
- Net: Only 211.5M tokens available for users to hold/trade
- Result: Supply/demand imbalance → price pressure UP

### Scenario: 500 Developers at Scale

**Year 2+ Calculation:**

```
500 developers × 110K tokens/month × 12 months = 660M tokens/year
```

**Problem:** Only 400M user reward tokens exist. Developers need 660M.

**Solution:** Developers buy from secondary market (DEX, exchanges)
- Creates buying pressure on open market
- Users (token hodlers) benefit from price appreciation
- High staking yields (20-50% APY) attract more developers who want to stake

## Pricing Examples at Scale

### 100 Developers, 500K DAU, 400M Tokens Circulating

**Annual developer spending:**
```
100 developers × $11K/month × 12 = $13.2M/year
In tokens: $13.2M ÷ token price = annual token demand
```

**At $0.10/token:** Developers demand 132M tokens/year
**At $0.50/token:** Developers demand 26.4M tokens/year
**At $1.00/token:** Developers demand 13.2M tokens/year

**Equilibrium:**
- User supply: 400M tokens issued (or less if staked)
- Developer demand: ~50M tokens/year
- Speculation demand: ~50M tokens/year
- Staking supply removed: ~200M tokens locked
- Result: Price settles where supply/demand balance

**Realistic price range at 100 developers:** $0.50-2.00/token

### 500 Developers, 5M DAU, 400M Tokens Circulating

**Annual developer spending:**
```
500 developers × $132K/year = $66M/year
In tokens at $1.00: 66M tokens/year demand
```

**Supply/demand ratio:**
- Supply: 400M (or less if staked)
- Demand: 66M + user staking + speculation
- Imbalance: Demand growing faster than supply
- Result: Strong price appreciation to $2-5/token
